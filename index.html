About Me

I'm a junior at Colby College, pursuing Computer Science major and Economics minor. I have industrial experience in Machine Learning (specialized in Computer Vision), DevOps Engineering, Data Engineering, and Software Engineering. I see myself as a driven, proactive, and accountable individual with an eagerness for knowledge and high adaptability. I love to explore the Tech industry further in different sub-areas to diversify my technical background.


Growing up in a developing country and being engaged in a liberal art education, I have realized that for every beneficiary of technological innovations, there are individuals left behind. My career goal is to develop and combine my technical expertise from Computer Science and social understanding from Economics to craft solutions to real-world problems and better people’s life, especially for those who are still left out by the technological advancement.
